About Kannada Service

We are a Christ-centered church where the Gospel of Jesus Christ is proclaimed with truth and love. As part of Calvary Reformed Church, we gather to worship God, grow in His Word, and serve our community with compassion. Our heart is to glorify God in all we do and to help people find hope and new life in Christ.

About Us

Calvary Reformed Church was established in 2015 by Rev. M Lazarus, with a vision to stand firmly on the truth of God's Word and to spread the light of the Gospel in our community and beyond. We are a family of believers who share life together through worship, prayer, discipleship, and service. Our ministry continues to be a source of spiritual encouragement and a beacon of hope, pointing people to Christ as the only Savior and Lord.


Our Ministries

At India Calvary Ministries, our heart is to serve both the church and the community. We provide a variety of ministries that nurture spiritual growth and encourage active service. Our youth programs inspire the next generation to follow Christ with passion and purpose, while our community service initiatives extend the love of God to those in need. Through Bible study groups, we equip believers with a deeper understanding of God's Word, building strong foundations for a faithful Christian life.


Our Worship Services

Sunday Kannada Worship

Every Sunday we gather at 10:30 am until 12:30 pm as a family of believers to worship the Lord in spirit and truth. Our services are centered on Christ through heartfelt praise, prayer, the reading of Scripture, and the faithful preaching of God’s Word.

Youth Ministry

We have youth fellowship at 9 am till 10 am every Sunday. And daily youth fellowship at 8 pm everyday in Channasandra, Empowering the next generation through faith.

Sunday School

We have sunday school for kids from age 4-12 every sunday at 9:30 am to 10:15 am. Fun, safe, and engaging Bible lessons for kids of all ages.
